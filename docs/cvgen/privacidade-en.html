Privacy Policy — CVGen

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes how Orbit Apps (“we”, “us”, or “Operator”) processes personal data in connection with the CVGen mobile application (the “App”). We are committed to transparency and compliance with the General Data Protection Law (Law No. 13.709/2018 — LGPD) and other applicable rules.

Contact (Data Protection): contact@orbitapps.dev

1. Who this Policy applies to

This Policy applies to Users of CVGen (individuals who install, register, or use the App), including free and paid plans.

2. Data Controller

Orbit Apps is the controller of personal data processed for the purposes described here, except where we act strictly as processor on behalf of third parties (e.g. infrastructure providers), as indicated in section 7.

3. What data we collect and why

We collect only what is necessary for the App to function, improve the experience, and comply with legal obligations. The main categories are:

Category Examples Purpose / legal basis (LGPD)
Account and authentication Email, password hash (we do not store plain passwords), session tokens, user ID. Account creation, login, security, fraud prevention. Legal bases: contract performance; legitimate interest (security).
Résumé content Text, sections, dates, education, experience, skills, optional photo, and other fields you fill in. Provide editing, PDF export, and optional cloud sync. Legal bases: contract performance; consent when required (e.g. sensitive data, if any).
Usage and diagnostics App version, device type, crash logs, basic performance metrics (when analytics is enabled). Stability, debugging, product improvement. Legal bases: legitimate interest; consent when required by platform or law.
Subscriptions and billing (via stores) Identifiers and status provided by Apple/Google/RevenueCat (we do not process full card data in the App). Manage plans and entitlements. Legal bases: contract performance; legitimate interest.
AI suggestions (Pro+) Excerpts of résumé text sent to processing through our secure functions. Generate suggestions to assist editing. Legal bases: contract performance; consent when applicable.
Communications Email address for support and, if applicable, service messages. Respond to requests and send necessary notices. Legal bases: contract performance; legitimate interest (support).

Sensitive data: Unless you voluntarily include sensitive categories in your résumé (e.g. health-related information), we do not request such data. If included, processing will be limited to providing the service you requested and based on applicable legal bases (including specific consent when required).

4. How we process AI suggestions

When you use AI features, excerpts of your text may be sent to models via Operator-controlled infrastructure. We apply measures to minimize data sent and to protect in transit and at rest. Outputs are suggestions only; you should review before publishing or exporting your résumé.

5. Cookies and similar technologies

The native App does not use browser cookies in the traditional sense. We may use SDKs and local storage for session, preferences, and diagnostics, according to platform policies and your settings.

6. Sharing of data

We may share data with:

  • Cloud infrastructure and database (e.g. Supabase): hosting, authentication, and sync;
  • Subscription management (e.g. RevenueCat) and app stores (Apple/Google): billing and entitlements;
  • AI providers via our secure layer: only what is necessary for suggestions;
  • Analytics/crash tools: if enabled, in aggregated or pseudonymized form where possible;
  • Authorities: when legally required or to protect rights.

We do not sell your personal data.

7. International transfers

Some providers may process data in other countries. When applicable, we adopt appropriate safeguards (e.g. contractual clauses, adequacy decisions) and comply with LGPD requirements.

8. Retention

We retain data while your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter for legal obligations, dispute resolution, and security. You may request deletion subject to legal retention (see section 10).

9. Security

We adopt technical and organizational measures appropriate to the risk, such as encryption in transit, access controls, and least privilege. No system is 100% secure; we encourage strong passwords and device updates.

10. Your rights (LGPD)

Depending on the case, you have the right to: confirm processing; access; correct incomplete or inaccurate data; anonymize, block, or delete unnecessary or excessive data; portability; information on sharing; revoke consent when processing is based on consent; and petition to the National Data Protection Authority (ANPD).

Requests: contact@orbitapps.dev. We will respond within reasonable deadlines and may request identity confirmation.

11. Children and adolescents

The App is aimed at adults capable of entering into contracts. We do not knowingly collect data from children without appropriate legal basis and consent of holders when required.

12. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy by publishing a new version with a “last updated” date. Material changes may be communicated via App or email when appropriate.

13. Contact

Privacy questions: contact@orbitapps.dev
